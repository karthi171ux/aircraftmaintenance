Beechcraft bonanza landing gear maintenance

Beechcraft Bonanza gear maintenance can be a bit complicated due in part to the fact that parts are either hard to get or simply do not exist at all. The wing hinge system is a primary issue, as it is more than likely rusted and will require overhauling before you even think of flying again.
The gear is self-retracting and can be retracted at up to 150 feet above ground level. The landing gear has two sets of wheels with three folding struts each for redundancy. When inspecting the struts make sure that there is no movement, or the strut washers will need to be replaced. Some struts also have bolts holding them together that may need tightening as well.
